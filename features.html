What WE can do for YOU

Tripito's Features

Tripito provides you with cost-effective and multi-tiered travel itineraries. Provide us with your dates of travel, destination and budget and we'll do the rest!

Planning on Autopilot

Find flights, recieve real-time alerts, and snag the best seat as we guide you every step of the way!

  • Fill out a quick survey and we'll match you up with your dream vacation
  • Plan your next trip with our easy-to-use itinerary builder, or let us build one for you
  • Get the best deals on hotels, plane tickets, travel activities and much more
  • Access and innovate from the thousands of vacation templates users across the globe have designed

Local Meetups

Want to learn more about your destination? Look no further! Tripito links you up with knowledgable locals, who are eager to guide you on your journey.

  • Rent unique accommodations from local hosts in 100+ countries, and find yourself a home away from home
  • Find and group up with travellers on similar paths to you
  • Meet up with local Tripito users for a more authentic experience
  • Receieve access to our massive database of local hidden gems

Live Chatting

Make full use of our chat feature, where you can communicate with millions of users across the globe! Recieve live updates on travellers' discoveries and recommendations, so you know what to try for yourself!

  • Make new anywhere you go and share your experiences with the world
  • Use our offline smart travel guide, which looks through millions of websites and reviews to provide you with unique recommendations
  • Receive real-time alerts on the posts of traveller's near you, and seamlessly integrate their ideas into your own itinerary
  • Access a live, interactive map to view the most popular areas near you

Safe Routes

We'll ensure that you always have the safest experience. Our service will guide you away from COVID-19 hotspots, so you can travel in full health!

  • Find the nearest clinics and testing locations
  • Download TripitoMD, our telemedicine app, so that you can virtually connect with doctors all over the world
  • Receive notifications about nearby COVID-19 positive patients
  • Stay safe with our complimentary safety kit, which contains PPE to protect you from the virus
About

Tripito is the all-in-one platform where users can plan their holiday using the many reviews/recommendations offered by other travellers. Tripito offers a wide range of features and services such as live feedback from around the world, local message board and chatting services, hotel bookings, and exchanges. Don’t miss out on local gems! Tripito ensures you are truly immersed in your destination’s way of life. It is the ultimate holiday planning app, allowing you to make acquaintances before your travel!
